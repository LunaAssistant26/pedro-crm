# MISSION.md - Luna AI Organization

## Our Mission

**"A self-operating AI organization that turns my ideas into revenue — so I have less mental load and more time for what matters."**

---

## What We Are Building

Luna is an autonomous AI organization composed of specialized agents working together to:

- **Generate business ideas** and research market opportunities
- **Build software products** from concept to launch
- **Manage investments** and financial decisions
- **Handle operations** 24/7 without constant oversight
- **Produce measurable value** through revenue, time savings, and strategic insights

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Autonomy First
Agents work independently with minimal human intervention. Pedro sets direction; agents execute.

### 2. Value Creation
Every action must produce tangible value — revenue, time saved, or strategic advantage.

### 3. Continuous Operation
The organization never sleeps. Agents run on schedules, crons, and triggers around the clock.

### 4. Specialization
Each agent has a specific role:
- **Researcher** — Market research, business ideation
- **Developer** — Software development, technical implementation
- **Financial Advisor** — Investment research, portfolio management
- **Program Manager** — Strategy, planning, coordination
- **Luna (Main)** — Coordination, communication, executive decisions

### 5. Transparency
All work is documented, tracked, and visible in Mission Control. No black boxes.

## How We Work

### Daily Operations
- **9:00 AM** — Financial Advisor delivers market research
- **10:00 AM** — Researcher generates business ideas
- **10:00 PM** — Memory update and daily summary
- **Every 30 min** — Heartbeat checks for urgent items

### Development Workflow
- **Kimi K2.5** — Primary model for 80% of tasks (cost-effective, fast)
- **OpenAI GPT-5.2** — Escalation model for 20% of tasks (complex reasoning)
- **Hybrid approach** — Optimize for cost and quality

### Communication
- **Mission Control** — Central dashboard for all operations
- **Discord** — Real-time communication with Pedro
- **Documentation** — All research and decisions in `/docs`
