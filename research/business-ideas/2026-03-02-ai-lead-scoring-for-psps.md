## Problem
Payment service providers (PSPs) waste significant resources manually qualifying high-risk merchant leads (gambling, crypto, FX). Current process is inefficient and misses opportunities.

## Solution
AI-powered lead scoring platform that:
- Scrapes public data to identify active high-risk merchants
- Scores leads by transaction volume, growth trajectory, and payment pain signals
- Provides PSPs with qualified, prioritized prospect lists

## Market Validation
- Pedro's existing network: 50+ PSPs actively seeking high-risk merchants
- Target customers: Payment orchestrators, acquirers, alternative payment method providers
- Pricing model: €2-5K/month per PSP + revenue share on closed deals

## Competitive Landscape
- Manual lead gen agencies (slow, expensive)
- Generic sales intelligence tools (Clearbit, ZoomInfo) — not focused on high-risk verticals
- No dedicated solution for payments industry

## Next Steps
1. Validate demand with 3-5 PSP contacts
2. Build MVP scraper for gambling operators
3. Test with Pedro's existing PSP network

## Risks
- Data scraping compliance (need legal review)
- High-risk merchants may not want to be identified
- PSPs may prefer existing relationships
