## 1. What Are Sweepstakes Casinos Doing?

### Core Mechanics
- **Dual Currency System:**
  - **Gold Coins (GC):** Purchased for entertainment, no cash value
  - **Sweeps Coins (SC):** Given free as bonuses, redeemable for cash prizes
- **"No Purchase Necessary" Legal Fiction:** Players can get free SC via mail-in requests (AMOE - Alternate Method of Entry)
- **The Loophole:** You're technically buying GC for fun; SC are "free gifts" that happen to be redeemable

### User Experience
- Sign up → Get free SC (typically 1-5 SC daily login bonus)
- Play slots/table games using SC
- Accumulate winnings → Redeem for cash after 1x playthrough
- Purchase GC packages (with bonus SC) when free coins run out

### Engagement Tactics
- Daily login bonuses
- VIP programs with rakeback
- Leaderboards and tournaments
- Referral bonuses (up to 20 SC per referral)
- Social media giveaways

## 5. The Crypto-Only Problem

### Why Crypto-Only Is Dangerous

**1. Regulatory Red Flag**
- Legit sweepstakes casinos accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, bank transfers
- **Crypto-only is viewed as a "shady" signal** by regulators and review sites
- "Shady sweepstakes casinos only accept crypto or Cash App" — SweepsKings.com

**2. AML/KYC Nightmare**
- Crypto increases money laundering exposure exponentially
- FinCEN increasingly scrutinizing crypto gambling
- Source of Wealth (SOW) checks nearly impossible with crypto
- Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) requirements create massive operational burden

**3. Market Access Limitations**
- Many users don't own crypto (especially casual players)
- Conversion friction: forcing users to buy crypto first = massive drop-off
- Crypto volatility creates UX problems ("I deposited $100 worth of BTC, now it's $80")

**4. Banking/Compliance Issues**
- Harder to find compliant payment processors
- Increased scrutiny from regulators
- Reputation risk with game providers (some may refuse to work with crypto-only sites)

### Existing Crypto Sweepstakes Players
- **Stake.us:** Crypto-only, but backed by billionaire Ed Craven and massive marketing budget
- **PlayFame:** Offers crypto + fiat (hybrid model)
- **SpeedSweeps:** Accepts BTC, ETH, LTC, DOGE

**Key Insight:** Even established crypto sweepstakes sites are seeing increased pressure. Stake.us is the exception, not the rule—and they have deep pockets for legal defense.
