## Business Idea #1: "The Safe Bet" — PipelineAI for Payments

### Problem Statement
High-risk merchants (gambling, crypto, CBD, adult) spend **3-6 months** finding a PSP that will board them. PSPs spend **$50K-$200K annually** on manual lead gen but still can't identify qualified high-risk merchants at scale. The matching happens through WhatsApp, Telegram, and conference introductions — completely unautomated.

### Solution
**PipelineAI** = AI-powered lead generation and qualification platform specifically for PSPs targeting high-risk merchants.

**Core features:**
1. **Merchant Discovery Engine** — AI scrapes public data (app store listings, LinkedIn, job posts, regulatory filings) to identify high-risk merchants actively scaling
2. **Risk Pre-Scoring** — Pedro's heuristics + public data = pre-qualify merchants before intro
3. **Warm Intro Automation** — Auto-generates personalized outreach via LinkedIn/email referencing Pedro's network connections
4. **PSP Matching Algorithm** — Matches merchants to Pedro's 50+ PSP network based on geography, vertical, volume

- **Revenue model fit:** Pedro already makes €2K-€10K/month per deal — this scales that engine

## Business Idea #2: "The Moonshot" — RiskStack (Tara Pivot for Acquirers)

### Problem Statement
Small-to-mid-size acquirers and PSPs (the ones Pedro works with) have **zero tooling** for merchant portfolio management. They use spreadsheets to track:
- Which merchants are approaching chargeback thresholds
- Rolling reserve release schedules
- Settlement delays and working capital constraints

66% of merchants face compliance challenges; 64% face settlement delays. PSPs lose money because they can't proactively manage risk.

### Solution
**RiskStack** = "Tara for Payment Acquirers"

A lightweight financial OS for small PSPs/acquirers to manage their high-risk merchant portfolios:
1. **Portfolio Dashboard** — Real-time view of merchant health (chargeback rates, volume trends)
2. **Cash Flow Forecasting** — Predict settlement delays, rolling reserve releases
3. **Alert Engine** — Notify when merchants hit risk thresholds
4. **Merchant Portal** — White-labeled interface for merchants to see their own metrics
